# DevTrader Content Guidelines

Daily education posts must be written for beginner Romanian traders who need clarity, discipline, and realistic expectations.

Rules:

- Romanian only for all visible Discord content.
- No profit promises or exaggerated claims.
- No direct financial advice.
- No trade signals.
- Do not tell members to buy, sell, enter, long, or short a specific trade.
- Always include risk context when discussing markets, setups, analysis, macroeconomics, or risk.
- Keep messages practical, concise, and readable on mobile.
- Write in a professional, human, calm tone.
- Avoid hype, fake urgency, luxury language, and pressure.
- Prefer exercises, questions, checklists, and reflection prompts over predictions.

Every post should help members understand process, not chase outcomes.
